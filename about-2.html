About SK Sangam Enterprises

One enterprise built on three crafts — construction, iron and clay — each held to the same standard of quality.

Company Overview

A Foundation Built Over 5+ Years

SK Sangam Enterprises began as a construction contracting business and grew into a diversified enterprise, adding iron stirrup manufacturing to supply reinforcement directly to sites, and mitti ka kulhar manufacturing to preserve a traditional craft for a new generation of customers.

Today we operate across three divisions with one shared promise: quality materials, skilled people, and work delivered on time.
